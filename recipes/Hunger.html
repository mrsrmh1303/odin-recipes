Rosemary Portabella Chicken

Description

A creamy and smooth date night meal (Hubby Approved!)

Perfect for making an impression without taking forever.. (Serves Two)

Ingredients

  • 2 large boneless skinless chicken breast halves
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 10oz baby portabella mushrooms
  • 4 tbsp butter, divided in half
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 0.5 cup chicken stock
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1.5 tsp fresh rosemary

Steps

  1. Season the chicken with salt and garlic powder
  2. Slice the mushrooms in half and set aside
  3. Heat a large pan over medium-high heat and add half the butter. Add the chicken and brown well on both sides, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Remove the browned chicken from the pan and set aside
  5. Add the remaining butter and add the mushrooms. Season with salt and cook for about 5 minutes or until the mushrooms are browned and softened
  6. Add the garlic and cook about 1 minute
  7. Add the chicken stock to deglaze the pan and bring to a boil
  8. Lower the heat to a simmer and add the rosemary. Cook the sauce until it redices by 1/3.
  9. Add the chicken and mushrooms back to the pan, cover, reduce heat to low and cook for 5-8 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through
